Sell Farm

If you want to finish the game, you can sell your farms at any time and receive the equivalent of 14 days of Cash. But you will no longer receive any passive income.
Example : If your farm is generating:
5,000/Hour x 24 Hours = 120.000 Cash/Day 120.000 x 14 Days = You Receive 1.680.000 Cash. ⚠️ But you will no longer receive any passive income
