📝 Audit 1 - (George Stamp)

​https://georgestamp.xyz/2022/11/sheep-land/​
As you can see from the audit performed by George Stamp, our Overall Score was 72. No SCAM Risk, Backdoors, Owner Privileges, or Other Risk.
The audit result just came out and here it is, we got a good result on all requirements except one, the FEES, and we want to explain in detail for everyone to be reassured that we resigned before we could change it, but we will leave all the information public for you.
So basically anyone who enters without a referral link, the manager's portfolio will receive the fee amounts, and this was a small mistake on our part, we admit it and because of this we have a low fee score, but there is an extremely simple solution, and it is already in action, we will leave this portfolio public for you . (Manager Wallet 👇)
​https://bscscan.com/address/0xc3aeD8C317284DE4B3a061bB6Afb63ecB56244E7​

So what will be done with the fees that this portfolio charges?

Very simple, we will inject it back into liquidity every week, and we will always pin the transaction on the group, so that everyone can check it out.
Regardless, we had a great result, it was just a small mistake with a solution that is already in place.
